About

What is paly.win?

This is a strealined version of the bloated Paly website site. It loads much faster and only includes links which are used on a daily basis.

Why is paly.win necessary?

paly.net is (1) slow to load, (2) not mobile-friendly, and (3) difficult to navigate. Paly.win fixes all of these problems. It's not intended to be a replacement for paly.net, but simply a place to access the school calendar and frequently-used links.

Why the name paly.win?

It's short and easy to remember.

Why is there no loading animation between pages?

Paly.win makes use of barba.js, a lightweight JS library for making transitions between pages faster and smoother. This helps make paly.win as fast as possible.
