What is paly.win?

This is a strealined version of the bloated Paly website. It loads much faster and only includes links which are used on a daily basis.

Why is paly.win necessary?

paly.net is (1) slow to load, (2) not mobile-friendly, and (3) difficult to navigate. paly.win fixes all of these problems. It's not intended to be a replacement for paly.net, but simply the site that you use regularly.

Why the name paly.win?

It's short, easy to remember, and hopefully evokes positive emotions.

Why the lack of custom fonts?

Sadly, custom fonts slow down sites considerably. In the effort to make paly.win as fast as possible, I decided against custom fonts and instead employed system fonts (my deepest apologies if you continue to use Internet Explorer).

Why is there no loading animation between pages?

paly.win makes use of barba.js, a lightweight JS library for making transitions between pages faster and smoother. This makes paly.win as fast as possible.
